COMPANY INTRODUCTION
Established in 2014, Seeviar strives to develop world class Augmented Reality (AR) solutions and devices for the consumer and business markets. The company is a market leader in its micro-optical display modules, location based digital and animation solutions, and low cost, light weight, large field of view AR glasses.
